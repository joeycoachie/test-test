Immediate Floor Corrections

01. Gate 4 Mandate: Eye Gaze

Where the eyes go, the spine follows. If a client's cervical spine drops during transitions, it is because their eyes dropped. Mandate: Dictate exactly where the client's eyes must look during every load transfer.

02. Spatial Command: The Middle Child

Do not let latecomers wander or back-row clients hide. Mandate: Within the first 5 minutes, visually sweep the room and deliver a distant verbal cue, by name, to the client furthest away from you. Own the room.

03. End of Class: The Zero-Spring Reset

After the KIN-013 Unwind, clients must wipe down the carriage. Mandate: All springs must be unhooked. Stopper and gear bar returned to neutral. Leave the floor perfectly reset for the next operator.

Rule: Keep the hips steady and level before moving the legs.

守则：在开始腿部动作前，保持臀部（骨盆）稳定中立且两侧等高。

Peraturan: Pastikan pinggul stabil dan seimbang sebelum menggerakkan kaki.

  • Make sure the client's lower back isn't aggressively arching off the mat.
  • The client should feel their deep stomach muscles working, not their lower back squeezing.
  • 确保客户的下背部没有过度拱起、完全脱离垫子。
  • 客户应当感受到肚脐深处的核心发力，而不是后腰肌肉的紧张挤压。
  • Pastikan bahagian belakang bawah pelanggan tidak melengkung secara agresif dari mat.
  • Pelanggan patut merasakan otot perut dalaman bekerja, bukannya belakang bawah yang tegang.

Target Elements

  • • Alignment of ASIS and Pubic Symphysis.
  • • Transversus Abdominis (TVA) co-contraction.

Milestones & Failures

  • → Fail: Tucking the pelvis (posterior tilt) to compensate for weak core loading.

Rule: Move the back smoothly, bone by bone.

守则：逐节、流畅地活动脊椎骨骼。

Peraturan: Gerakkan belakang dengan lancar, satu demi satu ruas tulang.

  • Cue them to imagine a pearl necklace lifting off a table one pearl at a time.
  • Watch out for clients just hinging up from their lower back.
  • 提示他们想象一串珍珠项链从桌上被逐颗提起的平滑曲线。
  • 微小关卡：谨防客户出现直接从后腰折叠、板块式平直起落的情况。
  • Bayangkan seutas rantai mutiara yang diangkat dari meja satu persatu.
  • Perhatikan jika pelanggan melentur terus dari bahagian bawah pinggang.

Target Elements

  • • Sequential segment-by-segment spinal mobility (flexion/extension).

Milestones & Failures

  • → Fail: Hinge points at the thoracolumbar junction. Spine moves as a single block.

Rule: Keep the sides of the waist equally long during side-work.

守则：侧向练习中保持侧腰等长，不可缩短塌陷。

Peraturan: Pastikan kedua-dua belah pinggang sama panjang semasa baringan sisi.

  • Don't let them collapse their ribs into the mat when lying on their side.
  • Keep a small "mouse house" space under their bottom waist.
  • 侧卧时，切勿让身体下侧的肋骨软塌塌地压实垫面。
  • 保持下方侧腰处有一条可以让"小老鼠穿过的小屋"般的悬空缝隙。
  • Elakkan rusuk jatuh ke mat semasa pelanggan berbaring di sisi.
  • Kekalkan ruang kecil di bawah pinggang sisi bawah (mouse house).

Target Elements

  • • Quadratus Lumborum (QL) and Oblique stabilization.

Milestones & Failures

  • → Fail: Dropping into the resting shoulder or resting hip joint.

Rule: Shoulders wide and down. The master switch for the neck is the Eye Gaze.

守则：双肩向下沉并向两侧展宽。颈椎的安全开关在于“视线控制”。

Peraturan: Bahu ditarik ke bawah dan melebar. Suis utama untuk leher adalah Arah Pandangan (Eye Gaze).

  • Dictate exactly where the client's eyes should look. Where the eyes go, the spine follows.
  • If eyes drop to the floor during a transition, the cervical spine drops, and the gate fails.
  • 明确指示客户眼睛应该看向哪里。视线看向何处，脊柱就跟随到何处。
  • 如果转换动作时视线掉向地板，颈椎就会塌陷，关卡即告失败。
  • Arahkan dengan tepat ke mana pelanggan patut memandang. Ke mana mata pergi, tulang belakang akan mengikut.
  • Jika mata tunduk ke lantai semasa transisi, tulang leher akan jatuh, dan kawalan gagal.

Target Elements

  • * Serratus anterior and lower trapezius dominance.
  • * Deep cervical flexor activation via occulo-motor reflex.

Milestones & Failures

  • → Fail: Cervical spine compression due to upper trapezius hyper-activation and dropped eye gaze.

Rule: Use the footbar and straps to create joint stability.

守则：巧妙利用脚杆、弹簧阻力和拉手产生深层关节的向心挤压和稳定性。

Peraturan: Gunakan pemijak kaki dan tali untuk kestabilan sendi.

  • Clients shouldn't lock out their knees or elbows sharply.
  • Use the springs to guide the movement, don't just let the springs throw them around.
  • 严禁客户猛烈地锁死膝盖或手肘关节。
  • 在向心和离心阶段均用肌肉控制阻力，绝非任由弹簧将肢体弹甩回去。
  • Elakkan pelanggan mengunci mati lutut atau siku mereka secara mendadak.
  • Gunakan spring untuk mengawal pergerakan, bukan ditarik kasar oleh spring.

Target Elements

  • • Co-contraction of muscles surrounding active joints to eliminate shear.

Milestones & Failures

Rule: The mandatory 5-minute protocol to decompress the spine and flush the joints.

守则：在每堂课结尾强制执行 5 分钟的脊柱释放和关节冲洗阻力方案。

  • 引导他们进行 Unwind（卸载压力）方案的六项经典体位拉伸。
  • Turunkan nada suara anda ("Late Night DJ") untuk menenang sistem saraf.
  • Bimbing mereka melakukan 6 gerakan regangan Unwind dengan teliti.

The Academy & System FAQ

Core system methodology, class taxonomy mapping, and situational protocols.

Client-Facing Class Explanations (Taxonomy)

1. Optimum Control (Level 1)

"What is this class?"

Plain English: "This is our foundation. It's on the Mat, Chair, or basic Reformer. It’s about slowing down, finding your neutral pelvis, and connecting with your deep core. It's perfect if you're new, recovering, or want to perfect your form before adding heavy resistance."

2. Optimum Capacity (Level 2)

"What is this class?"

Plain English: "This is where we add athletic load. We use the Reformer's spring tension to build muscular endurance and functional strength. Expect to sweat and test your limits, but we keep the movements controlled and safe."

3. Optimum Flow State (Level 3)

"What is this class?"

Plain English: "This is kinetic harmony. We link complex movements together continuously with very few breaks. It's an advanced class focused on rhythm, efficiency, and total body integration."

Protocol: Handling Privacy Complaints (The Glass Room)

If a client expresses discomfort with the see-through glass panels, do not apologize or promise frosted glass. We operate in transparency.

I’m going to put you right here in Lane X—it's our stealth spot where you have total privacy from the window. You're safe with me."
